San Diego Energy Overview

R Shiny Application
www.energydataexp.com

This is an interactive tool built using R to visualize the different energy loads in the San Diego region. You can get a quick snapshot of the region's renewable energy adoption progress (namely solar PV). Users can also take a closer look at the energy load patterns in the region by filtering load data based on the type of consumer (e.g., residential, commericial, etc.) and time of the year (e.g., month, weekday/weekend). Furthermore, there are advanced mapping capability to show detailed info based on zip code.
